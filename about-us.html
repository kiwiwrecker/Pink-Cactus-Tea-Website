About Us

Pink Cactus Tea is a locally owned boba tea shop here in the valley, who caters to both locals and tourists. We offer various teas, snacks, and different events for everyone to enjoy! Come on by and try out our delicious treats and more!

Mission Statement

At Pink Cactus, we serve the highest quality bubble tea by crafting every drink with premium ingredients, passion, and transparency. We also strive to add smiles to every cup by providing a welcoming space where friends, family, and community connect.
